About RIC

The Robotics Intelligence Centre (RIC) is a premier research institution dedicated to advancing the frontiers of intelligent robotic systems. Founded in 2015, we bring together world-class engineers, computer scientists, and AI researchers to develop robots that can perceive, learn, and adapt to complex real-world environments.

Our work spans four core areas: industrial automation robots that enhance manufacturing efficiency, service robots that assist in healthcare and domestic environments, autonomous mobile robots for logistics and transportation, and cutting-edge research in robotic cognition and human-robot interaction.

What sets RIC apart is our unique integration of neural networks with robotic control systems, enabling our creations to continuously improve through machine learning. Our 20,000 sq. ft. facility houses state-of-the-art testing environments, including a fully equipped industrial testbed, domestic living lab, and outdoor mobility course.

We collaborate with leading universities and industry partners worldwide to ensure our innovations translate into practical solutions. At RIC, we're not just building robots - we're developing the intelligent systems that will power the factories, hospitals, and homes of tomorrow.
